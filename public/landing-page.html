How It Works

From Estimate to Completed Job in One Connected Flow

Service Pro 911 guides your team from estimate or agreement creation through payment, dispatch, job completion, and customer feedback.

1

Create Estimate or Agreement

Create an estimate or agreement for the customer and prepare the work for approval.

2

Collect Payment

Accept payment from the customer once the estimate or agreement is approved.

3

Convert to Invoice

Convert the approved estimate or agreement into an invoice with a single step.

4

Convert Invoice to Job

Turn the invoice into a job so the work is ready for scheduling and field execution.

5

Assign Employee & Schedule

Assign the job to an employee and add the job date and time for the visit.

6

Enter Job Location

Add the job location so it can be displayed directly on the map for easy routing.

7

Mark In Progress On Site

When the employee arrives on site, update the job status to In Progress.

8

Complete Job & Send Feedback

Mark the job as completed after the work is finished and send the feedback form.
