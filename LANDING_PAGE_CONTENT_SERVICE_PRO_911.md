## Section: All-in-One Business Management

### Jobs and Scheduling
Create, assign, reschedule, and manage jobs with ease. Keep technicians and office staff aligned with a clear view of appointments, job progress, and upcoming work.

### Estimates, Invoices, and Agreements
Create professional estimates, convert them into invoices, and manage recurring service agreements in one workflow.

### Customer Management
Keep customer details, service history, notes, and related documents organized so your team always has the right context.

### Payments and Refunds
Accept payments, track status, and manage refunds with better visibility into invoice totals, item-level refunds, and payment history.

### Team Coordination
Support office teams and field technicians with tools that improve communication, assignment tracking, and job execution.

### Maps and Route Visibility
Use built-in maps support to quickly open service locations and view routes. Teams can navigate to job sites faster and reduce delays with easy route access directly from job details.

### Business Visibility
Get a better view of your operations with reports, dashboards, payment tracking, and document status monitoring.
