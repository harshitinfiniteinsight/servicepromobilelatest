# Service Pro Mobile App - All Updates

**Date:** February 5, 2026  
**Last updated:** February 5, 2026  
**Purpose:** A clear record of the changes made in the app

---

## 1. Link Existing Documents to Jobs

**What changed**
- You can now connect existing invoices, estimates, or agreements to any job.
- A new option appears in the job menu to connect a document.
- The label changes based on how the job was created:
  - From invoice → “Associate Invoice”
  - From estimate → “Associate Estimate”
  - From agreement → “Associate Agreement”

**How it works**
- Open a job card menu.
- Choose the associate option.
- Pick from existing documents.
- Only the relevant document type is shown.
- Documents already connected show a “Linked” badge.

**Why this matters**
- Keeps all related documents in one place.
- Makes it easier to connect past work to a job.
- Reduces the need to create new documents when one already exists.

---

## 2. Job Details View Clean‑up

**What changed**
- The “Associated Documents” section was removed.
- Invoice information now appears only in Items and Payments.
- Both sections show the full list of invoices connected to the job.

**Items section**
- Shows every invoice connected to the job.
- Each invoice can be expanded to see its items and totals.

**Payment section**
- Shows each invoice with its payment status.
- Shows the payment method for paid invoices.

**Why this matters**
- Less clutter on the job page.
- Easy to see what was billed and what was paid.
- No duplicate sections.

---

## 3. Service Photos: Higher Limits

**What changed**
- You can now add up to 20 photos in “Before Service.”
- You can now add up to 20 photos in “After Service.”

**Why this matters**
- More room to document complex jobs.
- Better visual records for customers and staff.

---

## 4. Service Photos: Mobile‑Friendly Layout

**What changed**
- The photo upload screen now uses tabs:
  - “Before Service”
  - “After Service”
- Only one section shows at a time.
- Tabs show how many photos are already added.

**Why this matters**
- Less scrolling on phones.
- Faster switching between before and after photos.
- Cleaner and more focused screen.

---

## 5. Photo Upload Guidance

**What changed**
- A short message appears in each photo section:
  “For best performance, upload images up to 2 MB each.”

**Why this matters**
- Clear guidance without blocking uploads.
- Helps keep uploads fast and smooth.

---

## 6. Set Up ACH First

**What changed**
- The app now guides users to complete ACH setup before moving forward with related steps.

**Why this matters**
- Clearer order of setup.
- Fewer issues later in the process.

---

## 7. Estimate Becomes Invoice When Paid

**What changed**
- When an estimate is paid, it automatically turns into an invoice.

**Why this matters**
- Less manual work.
- Keeps records accurate and consistent.

---

## 8. Reschedule Option on Scheduled Route Screen

**What changed**
- A reschedule option is now available directly in the Scheduled Route screen.

**Why this matters**
- Easier to adjust jobs without leaving the schedule view.
- Saves time for dispatchers and field staff.

---

## Summary

These updates make the app easier to use on mobile, improve how documents connect to jobs, simplify job details, and make photo uploads clearer and more flexible. The overall goal is to save time and reduce confusion while keeping everything organized.
